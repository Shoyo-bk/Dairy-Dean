📋 Park's Standards

Your guide for every zone — read before playing!

🌾 Zone 1 — Hygiene

  • Milk from healthy udders only
  • Sanitary animal premises essential
  • Handlers must be disease-free
  • Use sterile, tight-lidded pails
  • No carriers of typhoid / streptococcal disease

🔵 Zone 2 — MBRT

  • <30 min decolorisation → Very Poor 🔴
  • 30–120 min → Poor 🟠
  • 2–5 hrs → Satisfactory 🟡
  • >5 hrs (stays blue) → Good/Excellent 🟢

🔥 Zone 3 — Pasteurisation

  • Holder/LTLT: 63–66°C for 30 min
  • HTST: 72°C for 15 sec
  • Cool to <5°C immediately after
  • Overheating (>72°C) → nutrient loss

🔬 Zone 4 — Quality Lab

  • Phosphatase: Negative (clear = pass ✅; yellow = fail ❌)
  • SPC: ≤ 30,000/mL
  • Coliform: Zero/mL
